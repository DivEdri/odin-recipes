Best Way To Cook A Steak At Home

Ingredients

  • salt
  • pepper
  • fresh steak
  • butter

How To Cook ?

  1. put a pan on the fire on high flame
  2. wait until the pan is sizzling hot
  3. put salt and pepper on the steak
  4. put the steak on the pan for 15 seconds on each side
  5. lower the temparture and cook the steak to the deistred temparture
