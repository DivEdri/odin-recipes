How To Make The Best Rice Ever

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Canola oil

How To

  1. pour canola oil in the pot and boil for 3 minutes
  2. add rice and and stir for two minutes
  3. add water (if added one cup of rice add 1.5 cups of water)
  4. put the pot on low flame after water boils and cover the pot
  5. let it cook for 15 minutes
  6. keep the lid coverd for five minutes
  7. Bon appetit !!!
